## Understanding Amazon COSMO

COSMO leverages advanced AI to bridge the gap between customer searches and their actual needs:

### Core Components
- **Intent Recognition**: Advanced understanding of customer search patterns
- **Contextual Analysis**: Real-time processing of user behavior and preferences
- **Knowledge Graph**: Vast network of product relationships and use cases
- **Behavioral Learning**: Continuous adaptation to customer interactions
- **Smart Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions based on intent

### Key Capabilities
- Natural language understanding
- Context-aware search results
- Behavioral pattern analysis
- Real-time intent mapping
- Personalized product matching
